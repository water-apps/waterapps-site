Common Questions

Answers that usually come up in the first conversation

Do you work with existing toolchains?

Yes. Most enterprise environments have multiple CI/CD tools and legacy constraints. WaterApps focuses on improving flow and compliance without forcing a full rebuild.

Can you help with audit readiness?

That's a core strength. We design traceability, evidence capture, and control enforcement into delivery pipelines—so audit becomes "export and explain," not panic.

What does a typical first engagement look like?

Usually a short discovery + assessment, then phased delivery. You'll get a clear plan, quick wins early, and a roadmap for sustained improvement. Most clients see value in weeks, not months.

Do you work with government agencies?

Yes—WaterApps is registered on AusTender and NSW BuyICT. We understand government procurement, security requirements, and can scale through trusted subcontractors when needed.

Can you scale for larger engagements?

Absolutely. WaterApps maintains a pool of vetted DevOps, cloud, and AI engineers who can be brought in under a master subcontractor agreement. You get specialist depth without the overhead.

How do AI agents fit into a regulated engagement — and what happens to our data?

AI agents are used for tasks like code generation, test writing, documentation, and analysis — with the principal reviewing and approving every output before it touches your environment. No client data is used to train any model. All tooling is disclosed upfront, scoped to your approved platforms, and operated to meet your data residency requirements. We can provide a written tooling disclosure as part of engagement scoping.

What does handoff look like at the end of an engagement?

Every engagement includes a structured knowledge transfer: documentation, runbooks, architecture decision records, and a handover session with your team. We don't leave behind black-box systems. For ongoing programmes, a fractional advisory arrangement can maintain continuity without a full-time commitment.

What are your insurance and compliance credentials?

WaterApps carries $20M Professional Indemnity and $20M Public Liability insurance. We hold an active ABN (63 632 823 084), are registered on AusTender and NSW BuyICT panels, and can provide certificates of currency and capability statements on request. NDA and engagement agreement templates are available before any scoping conversation.
